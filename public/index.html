Key Insights

Agent-to-Agent is the Growth Engine

The majority of volume growth in Dec 2025 - Feb 2026 has been driven by agent-to-agent transactions, where buyer agents autonomously purchase data, compute, and security services from seller agents. This is especially visible in the Solana migration, where ~72% of volume is agent-to-agent services.

Micropayments Dominate

Average transaction size is ~$0.31, down from $0.66 earlier. This signals healthy maturation: agents are making more frequent, smaller payments for granular services (per-API-call, per-inference, per-data-point) rather than large batch purchases.

47% of Transactions are Artificial

Nearly half of all x402 transactions are from teams gaming leaderboards, but these represent only 14% of dollar volume. The 53% of legitimate transactions come from high-frequency services like AIsa, AgentLisa, and Heurist processing real micropayments.

Solana is Catching Up Fast

Base dominated Oct-Nov (86% of tx, 89% of volume), but Solana surged to ~36% of transactions and ~72% of volume by December, driven primarily by agent-to-agent services migrating for lower fees and faster settlement.

Facilitator Decentralization

Dexter overtaking Coinbase as the #1 facilitator shows healthy decentralization. Four facilitators now each process 10M+ transactions. Open facilitators like PayAI and community-run options are gaining share.

Sellers Provide Real Utility

Top sellers offer tangible services: smart contract auditing (AgentLisa), web scraping (Firecrawl), blockchain intelligence (Einstein AI, Heurist), LLM inference (BlockRun, Spraay). This is a genuine machine economy, not speculation.
